About

I am a PhD student at Caltech, advised by Pietro Perona. I did my BS and MS at UCSD where I was advised by Serge Belongie. My research work focuses on efficient dataset collection and fine-grained visual categorization, particularly for the natural world. I work closely with iNaturalist and the Cornell Lab of Ornithology where I help put my research into production.
