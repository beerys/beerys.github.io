About

I am working on my PhD at Caltech, advised by Pietro Perona. My PhD is funded by an NSF Graduate Research Fellowship. I did my BS at Seattle University where I was advised by Agnieszka Miguel. My research work focuses on machine learning and computer vision for biodiversity monitoring, particulary for detection and recognition of animal species in challenging camera trap data at a global scale. I work closely with Microsoft AI for Earth where I help put my research into production.

I'm an ex-professional ballerina, ask me for dance recommendations!
